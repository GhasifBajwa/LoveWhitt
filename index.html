Love Whitt!

That's right — This service is for YOU, the parents!

Once your little one arrives, it’s easy for the focus to shift entirely to them, leaving you feeling a bit left out. But who’s looking out for you during these early days? After all, you’ve done the hard work of bringing life into the world and are now focused on keeping your newborn happy and healthy. Your well-being is just as important.

So, what's Love Whitt all about?

Modern Problems Call for Modern Solutions

Inspired by the Dutch kraamzorg service, which offers essential support to new parents, The Gems brings a fresh and inclusive twist to postpartum care.

We’re here for everyone—mums, dads, single parents, adoptive parents, same-sex parents, and anyone else starting this new journey.

Whether you need practical help or a friendly face to chat with and support your mental health, we’ve got you covered. We’re all about making sure every family feels supported and cared for during this special, yet often overwhelming, time.

Introducing The Gems

A Gem is a person, usually from your local area, who is trained to support you with your postpartum needs from birth until your baby is nine months old. They can assist you and your household by preparing healthy meals, emptying the dishwasher, changing the bed, doing laundry, cleaning bottles, and preparing feeds. And if you just need someone to sit with and keep you company, they can do that too! Whatever your needs are during this time, we are not here to judge - only to support. Every Gem is DBS-checked and trained by a team of newborn care specialists, including paediatricians and infant nutritionists. The first few months post-birth are a special but vulnerable time and your safety and comfort are our top priorities. You can trust that your Gem has the knowledge, skills and compassion to support you as you settle into your new family life.

How does it work?

Straightforward & Affordable

Booking a Gem is straightforward and affordable!

For evening or overnight support from 7pm to 9am, you pay £15 per hour, with the same minimum 4 hour booking.

What is the Fourth Trimester?
The fourth trimester is all about those first few months after birth when babies are adjusting to life outside the womb. For new parents, it can be a whirlwind of feeding, changing, and napping - sometimes leaving you feeling overwhelmed.
